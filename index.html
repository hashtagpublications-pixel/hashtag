About Us

Hashtag Publication is a specialist educational content studio based in West Bengal, dedicated to producing rigorously accurate, curriculum-aligned learning materials for India's schools.

We partner with publishers to create textbooks, workbooks, worksheets, question banks, and digital content for Classes 1 through 10 — ensuring every page reflects the spirit of NCF 2023 and NEP 2020.

Our bilingual capability in English and Bengali gives us a unique edge in the Eastern Indian educational market, where quality vernacular content remains scarce.
